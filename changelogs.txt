- **Registration System Changes:**
  - Users have the option to choose between phone number or email for verification during registration.
  - It is no longer mandatory to fill out the medical history form during registration. Users can provide this information through the chat system or manually edit it through their profile page.

- **Login System Change:**
  - Users can log in directly with OTP (One-Time Password) authentication, eliminating the need to remember passwords and simplifying the login process.
